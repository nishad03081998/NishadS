MY STORY

I’m a computer science engineering graguate. During my second semester of engineering, I happened to attend a workshop on User Interface Designing conducted by two of my college seniors. That was my first introduction to the field of Design! And I’ve never looked back since then.

I became an active member of the software design and development club of the college to learn and experience more about how it works in the design field. I started taking part in many designing competitions that were held in the college and slowly became part of the design community at college. I have completed 3 internships which sums my work experience to 1 year.

A little about my personal background; I am a Malayali, but born and bought up in Bengaluru. I can speak English, Malayalam, Kannada, Hindi and Tamil. Movies, music, PC games and going on bike rides have always been my stress busters. I also love socialising, so please do reach out if you want to discuss design, Tech, movies or entertainment :)
